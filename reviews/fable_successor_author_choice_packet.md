## 3. Design notes on the two policy cells with new mechanics

- **A3 failure semantics.** The packet fixes the policy skeleton the
  Q contract must instantiate: *launch = the instant attempt randomness
  exists*; before that instant nothing is charged and recovery is a
  signed pre-attempt process disposition (for the beacon variant, a
  pre-committed next-event ladder forbids beacon shopping); after that
  instant, unified charging governs and no redraw exists; the mechanism
  identity is part of the signed Q contract, changeable only by a
  signed pre-attempt amendment that consumes no attempt because no
  launch occurred; a standing fallback mechanism is forbidden. This
  adds no new charging law — it locates the charter's existing
  every-launch rule at a precise instant.
- **A4 dry-run boundary.** Envelope consumption keys on *real T
  worlds*: test-only-seed fixtures and harness smoke runs are free;
  training a registrable learner on real T worlds spends E1; canonical
  candidate registration spends E2; behavior-relevant edits create new
  candidates (charter §4) and spend E2 again. This keeps the envelope
  meaningful without taxing engineering hygiene.
